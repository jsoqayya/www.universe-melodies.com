üìö Research and Writing Methodology

This book's journey began with a moment of deep contemplation when I read Allah's words: "And there is not a thing except that it glorifies [Allah] by His praise" (Quran 17:44). I paused for a long time at this verse, and began to wonder: How does a stone glorify Allah? Is it real, actual glorification, or merely symbolic, metaphorical glorification as some interpreters suggest?

From this question, I embarked on an extensive research journey that spanned:

  • Quranic exegesis and Islamic scholarly heritage
  • Contemporary physics: quantum mechanics, string theory, and multiverse theories
  • Consciousness studies and philosophy of mind
  • Neuroscience and the mysteries of anesthesia
  • Biological sciences and intelligent molecular behavior
  • Artificial intelligence and the future of consciousness
